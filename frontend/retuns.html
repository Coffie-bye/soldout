Returns & Refunds

Our hassle-free return policy

Easy 30-Day Returns

Most items can be returned within 30 days of delivery for a full refund

Free Return Shipping

We provide prepaid return labels for most items

Quick Refunds

Refunds processed within 3-5 business days after we receive your return

How to Return an Item

  1. Initiate your return through your account or our Returns Center
  2. Print your return label and packing slip
  3. Pack your items securely in the original packaging if possible
  4. Ship your return using the provided label within 30 days
  5. Receive confirmation when we process your return

Refund Information

  • Refunds are issued to the original payment method
  • Shipping charges are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error
  • Sale items may have different return policies (see product details)
  • Gift returns receive store credit

Return Exceptions

Some items cannot be returned for hygiene or safety reasons:

  • Personal care items (earrings, cosmetics, etc.)
  • Customized or personalized products
  • Downloadable software products
  • Gift cards

Damaged or Defective Items

If your item arrived damaged or defective:

  1. Contact us within 48 hours of delivery
  2. Provide photos of the damage or defect
  3. We'll arrange for a replacement or refund

International Returns

For international orders:

  • Return shipping costs are the customer's responsibility
  • Customs fees are non-refundable
  • Allow additional time for international shipping
